about

hottee is a unique brand specializing in bespoke clothing and accessories that are color activated when exposed to UV rays. the products are screen printed and hand sewn in los angeles CA.

care

hottee products are handmade and should be cared for in the same way!

  • hand wash with mild detergent
  • dry flat in doors or in the shade. do not expose to UV when drying.
  • wear often and enjoy the sun! the more frequently the UV ink is expoded to UV rays the longer the effect will last.
  • do not put product in a dark place until the color effect has completely faded or the color will set.
